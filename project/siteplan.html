NetCom Coding Club

The name "NetCom" is short for “Networked Communication,” representing how coding connects people through technology. It's also the official name of the high school coding club. The addition of “Coding Club” makes the purpose clear, and the name is memorable, tech-savvy, and appealing to students.

for the NetCom Coding Club

The purpose of the site is to promote the NetCom Coding Club by providing information about what the club does, sharing upcoming events and meeting times, showcasing members and their projects, and encouraging new students to join. The site also serves as a central hub where current members can stay updated on club activities and learning topics like Python, HTML, and robotics.

for the NetCom Coding Club

1. What programming languages and tech topics does the NetCom Coding Club teach? This will guide content for the “What We’re Learning” section.
2. How can I join the NetCom Coding Club or get more information about meeting times? This will drive content on the contact page and events calendar.
